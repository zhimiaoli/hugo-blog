A bit about me

Hello! I’m Laura Kalbag, a British designer living in Ireland, and co-founder of Small Technology Foundation. We make an app called Better that protects you while you’re browsing the web. My book Accessibility For Everyone is available in paperback, ebook, and audiobook from A Book Apart.
